About Milan Papad

Founded in 1995 by a group of determined women in Gujarat, Milan Papad started with the aim of preserving the authentic taste of hand-rolled papads while empowering rural women. Today, Milan Papad has become a household name known for its unmatched crunch, flavor, and purity.

Our Mission

To promote traditional Indian snacks globally while ensuring quality, hygiene, and women empowerment at the grassroots level.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted brand in Indian households for authentic handmade snacks, supporting rural artisans and sustainability.
