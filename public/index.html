Our Legacy

Founded in 1995, Milan Papad started in a small village in Gujarat with a handful of women making papads using ancestral recipes. Over the years, our commitment to quality and tradition helped us grow into a trusted name in homes and stores across India. Today, we proudly combine handmade craft with modern practices while staying true to our roots.
